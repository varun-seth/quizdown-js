Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-03-04

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in quizhub for Web.

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information about you when you visit our website.

Google Drive Access

The application allows you to login with your Google account to view and edit files in your Google Drive. We do not have access to your Google Drive. Authentication is handled directly by Google, and only you can access your files through the app.

Consent

This app works by letting you access your drive files via this app. Only those files are accessible which you select using Google Drive picker in this app or whichever new files you create using this app. This app does not have an way to access "all files of your drive". This is clearly visible in the consent screen that appears on the first login. If you wish to revoke this consent, you can manually delete this app from your google account connections. https://myaccount.google.com/connections

Logout

After signing-in via Google, the authorization to access your drive files is kept only in your browser. Logging out will delete this authorization from your browser. Once logged out, this app does not have any access to your drive or any other data.

No Tracking

We do not track you. The app is hosted on GitHub Pages/Vercel and is purely a frontend application without any backend processing. No login or tracking mechanisms are implemented on our side.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google Drive API for accessing Google Drive files. We do not control the privacy practices of Google. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time, although it is highly unlikely. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
